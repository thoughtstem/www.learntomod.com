LearnToMod Development Update: March 25, 2015

March 25, 2015

Have you ever wanted to create your own dungeon in Minecraft? We’ve developed some new video tutorials that will help you make some awesome dungeons for your friends to explore. Here’s the first video of the three-part series:

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6IFTojk_qms

Check out the second and third videos too!
