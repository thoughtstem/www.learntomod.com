]]> The goal of the Tales of Now mod is to add a new level of adventure to the adventures already in Minecraft. Four new elemental ores have been added which allow the player to craft elemental weapons and armor for Fire, Water, Earth, and Wind.

An elemental ore can only be mined with a special elemental pickaxe, then dropping a crystal. These crystals will then turn into gems, that when crafted, produce powerful new armor or weapons for each element.

This is the first “client side” mod to be built 100% with LearnToMod, so we’re really proud of it! Check it out in this recording of Thursday’s Twitch stream:

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=AL1DG3k8WXE
